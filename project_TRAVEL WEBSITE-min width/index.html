Maldives

₹60,000

The Maldives is a tropical island country in the Indian Ocean, known for its clear blue water, white sandy beaches, and coral reefs. It is made up of many small islands and is famous for tourism, fishing, and luxury resorts.

Varansi

₹50,000

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world, located on the banks of the River Ganga in India. It is a major religious and cultural center, famous for its temples, ghats, and spiritual traditions.

Spiti Valley

₹70,000

Spiti Valley is a cold desert region in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its rugged mountains and Buddhist monasteries. It is famous for its peaceful landscapes, ancient culture, and high-altitude villages.

Goa

₹35,000

Goa is a coastal state in western India, known for its beautiful beaches, relaxed lifestyle, and rich Portuguese heritage. It is a popular tourist destination famous for its festivals, seafood, and historic churches.
